The mobile app graveyard is enormous. Most apps launched in India each year are abandoned within 90 days — not because the technology failed, but because the apps solved problems users didn't have, demanded engagement users didn't want to give, or buried value behind onboarding friction users wouldn't tolerate. Building an app that ships is easy. Building an app users actually use, return to, and recommend is the harder problem most agencies don't solve.

As a mobile app development company in India, DV Digital builds iOS and Android apps engineered for retention rather than just installation. We work across native development (Swift, Kotlin), cross-platform frameworks (React Native, Flutter), and hybrid architectures — choosing the right stack for actual product requirements rather than agency preference. From discovery through post-launch growth, our discipline is shipping apps that earn the home screen real estate they take up.

Our Mobile App Development Process

The native-versus-cross-platform debate has become more nuanced as cross-platform frameworks have matured. We make the choice based on actual product requirements:

Native (Swift/Kotlin) — when performance is paramount, deep platform integration is required, or specialised hardware (camera, AR, sensors) is core to the experience

React Native — when web and mobile share substantial logic, JavaScript expertise exists in the team, and time-to-market matters more than perfect-native polish

Flutter — when consistent design across platforms matters more than platform-native feel, and the team values productivity over fragmentation

Product strategy and user research — what problem, for whom, why an app

Information architecture and user flow design before any visual work

Wireframing and interactive prototyping for stakeholder alignment

High-fidelity design with platform-specific (iOS Human Interface, Material) considerations

Sprint-based development with weekly demos and feedback cycles

QA across device matrix — multiple iOS versions, Android OEMs, screen sizes

App Store and Play Store submission, ASO setup, and review response

Post-launch monitoring, analytics integration, and iterative growth

Native vs Cross-Platform — How We Decide

Hybrid (Capacitor, Ionic) — for content-heavy apps where mobile web and app should share most of the codebase

Progressive Web Apps — when distribution matters more than app store presence and offline capability is limited need

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does mobile app development take?
A standard consumer app takes 4 to 6 months from design to App Store/Play Store submission. Complex enterprise apps with backend integrations can take 6 to 12 months or more.
Do you provide post-launch support and updates?
Yes. We provide ongoing support and development retainers covering OS compatibility updates, performance monitoring, bug fixing, and new feature development.
Should we build native apps or use React Native / Flutter?
Depends on product requirements. Native (Swift/Kotlin) for performance-critical apps, deep platform integration, or hardware-heavy use cases. Cross-platform (React Native, Flutter) for time-to-market priority and shared logic across platforms. We recommend honestly based on actual requirements — we do both well, so we have no agency-preference bias.
What does mobile app development cost?
Simple consumer apps with standard features: ₹8–25 lakhs. Mid-complexity apps with backend integration: ₹20–60 lakhs. Complex apps with custom features, real-time, or high-performance requirements: ₹50 lakhs to several crores. Pricing depends on platforms (iOS only, Android only, or both), feature scope, and backend complexity. Custom proposals always after discovery.
Do you handle app store submission and updates?
Yes — App Store and Play Store submission is part of every project, including ASO (App Store Optimisation) setup, screenshot design, listing copy, and review response. Post-launch maintenance retainers cover ongoing updates, OS version compatibility, bug fixes, and feature additions. Apps without ongoing maintenance become obsolete within 12-18 months.
