DV Digital's custom software development team builds web applications, internal tools, and API integrations that are built precisely around your business requirements — not around a product roadmap you have no control over.

Off-the-shelf software solves the problems that are common across many businesses. Custom software solves the problems that are specific to yours — the operational quirks, regulatory requirements, integrations, and competitive advantages that no SaaS product was built to handle. For most businesses, off-the-shelf is the right answer. For some, the gap between what generic software does and what your business actually needs is the gap between profitable scale and operational chaos.

As a custom software development company in India, DV Digital builds bespoke software solutions for businesses that have hit the limits of off-the-shelf products. We have built CRM customisations, internal operational tools, customer-facing applications, integration middleware, and full enterprise software suites — and our discipline is building software that actually solves the business problem, not software that demonstrates technical sophistication.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you price custom software projects?
Custom software is scoped and priced based on a detailed requirements specification. We provide a fixed-price quote for well-defined projects and a time-and-materials arrangement for projects where requirements will evolve.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance after launch?
Yes. We provide ongoing support and development retainers covering bug fixing, performance monitoring, security updates, and new feature development.
Should we build custom or buy SaaS for our problem?
Almost always start with SaaS — off-the-shelf products are faster, cheaper, and better-supported than anything you build. Only consider custom when SaaS forces workflow compromises that materially hurt the business, when scale economics make per-user SaaS pricing prohibitive, or when functionality genuinely doesn't exist in any product on the market. Honest agencies steer you away from custom builds when SaaS would serve you.
What happens after the software is built?
Custom software needs ongoing operations — bug fixes, security patches, feature additions, infrastructure management, and adaptation to changing business needs. We offer maintenance retainers covering all of these, or we transition operations to your in-house team with documentation and training. Software without ongoing operations becomes liability software within 12-18 months.
