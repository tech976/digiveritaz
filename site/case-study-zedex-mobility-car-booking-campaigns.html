Automobile Mobility

Booking Cars at Scale

How Zedex Mobility built a predictable 81-booking monthly engine across Tata, Kia and Škoda — with a 200X peak-season ROAS.

Client
Zedex Mobility
Industry
Automobile Mobility
Services
Lead Gen, Bookings
Platforms
Meta, Google

The Challenge

Zedex Mobility runs sales and service centres for Tata Motors, Kia and Škoda Auto. They needed a digital engine that could deliver consistent bookings — not walk-in volatility — while keeping CAC under control.

  • Generate consistent car bookings through digital platforms
  • Reduce dependency on walk-ins
  • Improve lead-to-quality-lead ratio
  • Lower a high Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  • Build a predictable monthly booking flow

The Strategy

Google Ads — intent-based targeting with keywords like “Tata car booking near me”, “Kia test drive booking” and “Skoda showroom near me”. Brand + location segmentation, call-only and lead-form ads, PMax for showroom footfall, and tight negative-keyword filtering.

  • Meta Ads creative direction — festive offers, exchange bonus, zero down payment
  • Test-drive CTA creatives and carousel model showcases
  • Customer testimonials for trust-building
  • Scarcity messaging during peak months

Audience funnel: Awareness → Lead → Booking → Retargeting. Cold: auto intenders, competitor-interest, geo-radius. Warm: 30/60/90-day website visitors, 50%+ video viewers. Hot: previous leads and WhatsApp enquiry retargeting.

Booking Growth

Month 1: ~38 bookings. Month 2: ~52. Month 3: ~67. Stabilised monthly average: 81. Peak festive season: 110–125 bookings.

Blended revenue per car: ₹6.5L. Monthly revenue at 81 bookings: ~₹5.26 Cr. Peak-season revenue at 120 bookings: ~₹7.8 Cr+.

The Results

A predictable booking engine — with peak-season ROAS that would make most dealers double-take.
