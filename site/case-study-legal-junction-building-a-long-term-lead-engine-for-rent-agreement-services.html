A Long-Term Lead Engine

How we built a compounding, automation-led search engine for Legal Junction's rent-agreement services in Pune.

Client
Legal Junction
Industry
Legal Services
Services
PPC, WhatsApp Automation
Platforms

The Challenge

Legal Junction wanted more than a one-off lead burst. The goal was a system — one that captured high-intent users in Pune and set up recurring revenue through annual renewals.

  • Capture high-intent users searching for rent agreement services in Pune
  • Create a system built for recurring revenue via annual renewals
  • Run a performance-led, automation-powered funnel via Google Search

The Strategy

A tight, search-led build paired with WhatsApp automation for instant response — the combination rent-agreement intent demands.

  • High-intent keyword targeting on Google Search
  • Urgency-led ad copy aligned with user needs
  • Conversion-optimised landing pages
  • WhatsApp automation for instant lead engagement
  • Nurture flows built for long-term LTV

A search-plus-automation engine with strong unit economics.
