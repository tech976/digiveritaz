Quality Leads at Scale

How JK Shah Classes generated 1,500+ leads — with 80% marked high-quality — via multi-platform performance marketing.

Client
JK Shah Classes
Industry
Education
Services
Paid Social, PPC, Performance
Platforms
Meta, Google

The Challenge

JK Shah Classes — a leader in CA, CS, CMA and CFA coaching — needed a lead engine that didn't just generate volume, but delivered admission-ready aspirants.

  • Reach the right audience actively searching for professional courses
  • Generate high-quality leads with real conversion potential
  • Improve return on ad spend — impressions to admissions

The Strategy

A full-funnel, multi-platform system across Meta and Google built around precision, creative relevance and continuous iteration.

  • Precise audience targeting — demographics, interests, behaviours and lookalikes
  • Keyword-focused ad groups on Google for high-intent course searches
  • Impact-led creatives featuring faculty credentials and student success stories
  • Custom, mobile-first landing pages per campaign for faster lead capture
  • Continuous A/B testing and dynamic budget allocation

In Their Words

“A well-executed digital advertising campaign can transform brand visibility into tangible business growth.”

A predictable, quality-first lead engine — not just a burst of traffic.
