The Four Tiers of Creators We Work With

Different campaign objectives require different creator tiers. We help brands match the right tier to the right outcome instead of chasing follower counts.

03

Nano Creators (1K–10K followers)

Highest engagement rates, deepest community trust, and lowest cost per collaboration. Ideal for hyperlocal launches, niche categories, and authentic word-of-mouth at scale across multiple creators.

04

Micro Creators (10K–100K followers)

The workhorse tier of Indian influencer marketing — strong engagement, defined niche audiences, and proven conversion ability. Best for product launches, category education, and performance-driven campaigns.

05

Macro Creators (100K–1M followers)

Significant reach, polished content, and broader awareness drive. Suited for brand storytelling, category awareness, and amplifying campaigns alongside paid media.

06

Celebrity & Mega Creators (1M+ followers)

Mass-market awareness, news-worthy launches, and category-defining moments. We negotiate fair rate cards, manage usage rights, and ensure deliverables are met.

Different platforms drive different outcomes. Instagram remains the dominant influencer platform in India, but YouTube long-form is growing rapidly for high-consideration purchases, and LinkedIn creators are becoming critical for B2B.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What budget do I need to start influencer marketing?
Meaningful campaigns can start from ₹1.5–2 lakhs total — covering 8–15 nano/micro creators or 1–2 macro creators. Larger campaigns featuring celebrity tie-ups can range upwards of ₹25 lakhs. We recommend campaign size based on your objectives, not pushed budgets.
How do you verify a creator's followers are real and not bought?
Every creator we work with is screened using audience authenticity tools (HypeAuditor, Modash) plus manual review of engagement quality, comment-to-like ratios, follower growth patterns, and audience demographics. Creators failing these checks are removed from our database regardless of follower count.
Will the creators disclose paid partnerships as required by ASCI?
Yes — and this is non-negotiable. Every creator brief includes explicit ASCI disclosure requirements, and we audit posts after publishing to ensure compliance. This protects your brand from ASCI complaints and regulatory action while building genuine consumer trust.
Can I keep posting the influencer content on my own channels afterwards?
Yes — but only with proper usage rights, which we negotiate upfront in every contract. Standard rights typically include 30–90 days of organic reposting on brand-owned channels; paid usage rights for ads cost extra and are negotiated separately based on duration and scope.
Do you handle celebrity endorsements or just digital creators?
Both. We have negotiated and managed campaigns with film actors, sports personalities, and category-leading digital creators. Celebrity tie-ups require longer lead times (8–12 weeks), more rigorous contracting, and significantly higher budgets — but we have the network and process to execute them.
