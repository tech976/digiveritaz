How we work

From brief to attributed revenue — every campaign.

Five disciplines we run in lockstep so creator spend behaves like media spend — measurable, repeatable, and worth scaling.

01

Brief & campaign objectives

Every engagement starts with a campaign brief locked to clear objectives, KPIs and creator-fit criteria — reach, conversion, branded-search lift or category education. We define what success looks like before a single creator is approached, so reporting later has something honest to ladder back to.

02

Creator shortlisting & vetting

Shortlists drawn from our vetted database with engagement and audience authenticity reports (HypeAuditor, Modash) plus manual checks on comment quality, follower growth pattern and audience demographics. Creators failing these checks are removed regardless of follower count.

03

Negotiation, contracting & usage rights

Direct rate-card negotiation, deliverable scope locking and usage rights agreed upfront — including 30-90 day organic reposting and paid-amplification rights priced separately. No hidden middleman markup, no post-campaign surprises about ad usage.

04

Creator brief, ASCI disclosure & content review

Every creator gets a brief with explicit ASCI disclosure requirements, talking points and brand-safety guardrails. Content moves through approval cycles and revision management before posting — we never let creators publish on autopilot.

05

Posting coordination & ROI reporting

Coordinated posting windows, hashtag and tag verification, UTM-tagged link tracking, real-time performance monitoring and a post-campaign analytics report covering reach, engagement, link CTR, code redemptions and CPE / CPA versus paid-media benchmarks.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they brief a creator.

What budget do I need to start influencer marketing?
Meaningful campaigns can start from ₹1.5–2 lakhs total — covering 8–15 nano/micro creators or 1–2 macro creators. Larger campaigns featuring celebrity tie-ups can range upwards of ₹25 lakhs. We recommend campaign size based on your objectives, not pushed budgets.
How do you verify a creator's followers are real and not bought?
Every creator we work with is screened using audience authenticity tools (HypeAuditor, Modash) plus manual review of engagement quality, comment-to-like ratios, follower growth patterns and audience demographics. Creators failing these checks are removed from our database regardless of follower count.
Will the creators disclose paid partnerships as required by ASCI?
Yes — and this is non-negotiable. Every creator brief includes explicit ASCI disclosure requirements, and we audit posts after publishing to ensure compliance. This protects your brand from ASCI complaints and regulatory action while building genuine consumer trust.
Can I keep posting the influencer content on my own channels afterwards?
Yes — but only with proper usage rights, which we negotiate upfront in every contract. Standard rights typically include 30–90 days of organic reposting on brand-owned channels; paid usage rights for ads cost extra and are negotiated separately based on duration and scope.
Do you handle celebrity endorsements or just digital creators?
Both. We have negotiated and managed campaigns with film actors, sports personalities and category-leading digital creators. Celebrity tie-ups require longer lead times (8–12 weeks), more rigorous contracting and significantly higher budgets — but we have the network and process to execute them.
