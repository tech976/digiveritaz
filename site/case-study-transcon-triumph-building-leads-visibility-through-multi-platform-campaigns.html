Real Estate

Building Leads & Visibility

How a multi-platform campaign built high-intent pipeline and visibility for a premium residential launch.

Client
Transcon Triumph
Industry
Real Estate
Services
Paid Social, PPC, Video
Platforms
Meta, Google, YouTube

The Challenge

Premium real estate buyers take months to convert. Transcon Triumph needed both bottom-of-funnel lead volume and top-of-funnel brand trust — at the same time.

  • Strengthen digital footprint in a competitive Mumbai market
  • Generate high-quality leads from genuine buyer intent
  • Build trust and visibility among high-consideration prospects

The Strategy

A multi-channel play that matched each platform to its funnel strength.

  • Google & Meta for direct lead generation
  • YouTube for video-led awareness
  • Meta engagement campaigns for mid-funnel warm-up

The Results

A cost-efficient lead machine paired with brand-building at scale.
