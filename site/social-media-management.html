FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How many posts per month should my brand publish?
It depends on platform and category, but typical engagements include 12–20 Instagram posts, 8–15 Reels, daily stories, 8–12 LinkedIn posts, and weekly YouTube Shorts. We tailor cadence to what your audience and category actually rewards — not to padded post counts.
Do you provide content creation including video and design?
Yes — fully in-house. Our team includes graphic designers, video editors, copywriters, and short-form video creators. Most clients do not need to provide anything beyond product access, brand assets, and occasional founder time for talking-head content.
How do you handle negative comments or PR situations?
Every engagement begins with a defined response playbook covering complaint handling, escalation triggers, and crisis communication. Negative comments are answered promptly, professionally, and where appropriate moved to DMs — protecting your brand reputation while genuinely resolving customer concerns.
Can you grow my followers organically without running ads?
Yes, but with realistic expectations. Organic growth in 2026 is slower than three years ago because platforms now prioritise paid distribution. We deliver consistent organic growth through quality content, but combining organic with even a modest paid amplification budget delivers significantly faster compounding results.
What does a typical monthly social media management package cost?
Most packages range from ₹40,000 to ₹1,50,000 per month depending on platforms, content volume, and video production scope. We provide custom proposals after understanding your goals, current state, and creative requirements — so you pay for what you actually need.
