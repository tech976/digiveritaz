How we work

From strategy to scroll-stopping content — every month.

Five disciplines we run in lockstep so your handle compounds instead of just posting.

01

Content strategy & monthly calendar

Every engagement starts with audience research, competitor benchmarking and content-pillar definition. From there we build a 30-day calendar mixing brand storytelling, product education, social proof and platform-native trends. Every post serves a strategic purpose.

02

Creative production — fully in-house

Designers, video editors and copywriters under one roof produce static graphics, carousels, Reels, YouTube Shorts and long-form video. Everything is on-brand, platform-optimised, and shipped without outsourcing — meaning faster turnaround and tighter quality control.

03

Community management & response

DM responses, comment moderation and community engagement handled within defined SLAs. We protect your brand voice, escalate genuine queries to your team, and build the conversational presence that turns followers into customers.

04

Paid amplification (when it makes sense)

Organic distribution alone is slower than it was three years ago. We layer modest paid amplification on top of your best-performing organic content so the work compounds faster — without burning budget on whitewashed boost posts.

05

Monthly reporting & strategy review

Every month you receive a report covering engagement, reach, follower growth and conversion-tied metrics. We meet to review what worked, what didn't, and exactly how the next 30 days will be sharpened.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

How many posts per month should my brand publish?
It depends on platform and category, but typical engagements include 12–20 Instagram posts, 8–15 Reels, daily stories, 8–12 LinkedIn posts and weekly YouTube Shorts. We tailor cadence to what your audience and category actually reward — not padded post counts.
Do you provide content creation including video and design?
Yes — fully in-house. Our team includes graphic designers, video editors, copywriters and short-form video creators. Most clients need to provide nothing beyond product access, brand assets and occasional founder time for talking-head content.
How do you handle negative comments or PR situations?
Every engagement begins with a defined response playbook covering complaint handling, escalation triggers and crisis communication. Negative comments are answered promptly, professionally and where appropriate moved to DMs — protecting your brand reputation while genuinely resolving customer concerns.
Can you grow my followers organically without running ads?
Yes, with realistic expectations. Organic growth in 2026 is slower than three years ago because platforms now prioritise paid distribution. We deliver consistent organic growth through quality content, but combining organic with even a modest paid amplification budget delivers significantly faster compounding results.
What does a typical monthly social media management package cost?
Most packages range from ₹40,000 to ₹1,50,000 per month depending on platforms, content volume and video production scope. We provide custom proposals after understanding your goals, current state and creative requirements — so you pay for exactly what you need.
