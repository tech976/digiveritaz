FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

What ad spend do you recommend for Meta campaigns?
We recommend a minimum of ₹25,000 per month in ad spend to generate statistically meaningful data for optimisation. Higher budgets allow faster learning, broader testing, and more aggressive scaling.
Do you manage Instagram Reels ads?
Yes. We create and manage Reels ads as part of our Meta campaigns. Video-first formats including Reels frequently deliver the strongest engagement and conversion rates.
What's the minimum monthly Meta ads budget you manage?
We typically engage accounts spending ₹2 lakhs/month or more — below that, management fees become a disproportionate share of spend and machine learning needs more data than the budget can provide. Smaller budgets are better deployed in tightly-targeted Google Ads.
Do you set up Meta Conversion API for our account?
Yes — CAPI implementation is included in our setup process for every new client. Without CAPI, post-iOS 14.5 attribution is materially broken. CAPI implementation alone has improved reported ROAS by 25–40% for clients we have onboarded.
How long until Meta ads start delivering profitable ROAS?
Most well-set-up campaigns reach profitable ROAS within 3–6 weeks once creative testing identifies winning angles and the algorithm calibrates. Accounts with poor pixel/CAPI history take longer because Meta's learning data is corrupted and needs to be rebuilt.
