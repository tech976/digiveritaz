How we work

From angle to landed coverage — every month.

Five disciplines we run in lockstep so your brand earns coverage instead of just hoping for it.

01

Research & story development

Every engagement starts with category mapping, competitor coverage audits and angle discovery. We surface the data, founder narrative or contrarian POV that journalists actually want to write about — not boilerplate "company update" releases that get deleted on sight.

02

Journalist outreach & relationships

We don't blast wire services. We map the 3-5 reporters in each target publication who actually cover your beat, study their last six months of bylines, and craft outreach that respects their workflow. Relationships are the reason 6 of every 10 of our pitches land.

03

Pitching & founder positioning

Tight, news-pegged pitches sent at the right time of week to the right inbox — paired with founder thought-leadership pieces, expert quote placements and HARO submissions. Multiple pitch lanes running in parallel so coverage isn't a once-in-six-months event.

04

Coverage & verification

Every landed placement is verified live — public URL captured, backlink type confirmed (dofollow vs nofollow), domain authority logged, referring domain growth tracked. You see the receipts before we send the invoice.

05

Measurement & monthly review

Every month you receive a report tying placements landed → backlink value → referring-domain growth → branded search lift → AI-search citations. We meet to review what landed, what didn't, and exactly how the next 30 days will be sharpened.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they engage a PR partner.

How long does it take to land tier-1 coverage?
Realistic timelines are 6–10 weeks from engagement start to first major placement. The first month is research, story development and journalist outreach; second and third months produce placements as pitches land. Sustained PR retainers compound — by month 6, monthly coverage becomes consistent rather than episodic.
Can you guarantee placement in a specific publication?
No reputable digital PR agency guarantees specific placements — that would imply pay-for-coverage, which compromises editorial credibility. What we guarantee is a structured outreach process, qualified pitch volume, monthly placement targets and full transparency on what landed and why.
Do you do paid placements or only earned coverage?
We focus on earned editorial coverage. Sponsored content and advertorial placements are a separate, transparent service where you know the placement is paid — and the SEO and credibility value is fundamentally different. We never disguise paid placements as earned coverage.
How is digital PR priced?
Most digital PR retainers range from ₹75,000 to ₹3,00,000 per month depending on scope, target publications and whether founder-led campaigns and original research are included. We provide custom proposals after understanding your goals and audience.
Will digital PR help me rank for competitive keywords?
Yes, indirectly but powerfully. Editorial backlinks from DA 70+ publications materially lift domain authority — which is foundational to ranking for competitive keywords. Combined with on-page SEO and content marketing, digital PR is often the difference between ranking on page 2 and ranking on page 1.
