For brand awareness, retargeting and competitor conquesting, display offers unmatched reach at efficient CPMs — when the metrics that matter are reach, frequency, brand lift and view-through conversion, not raw clicks.

For most Indian brands, display works hardest as a remarketing channel — re-engaging warm audiences who already know your brand. As a display advertising agency in India, we engineer placements, frequency caps and creative that pay off in measurable downstream conversion, not impression-counting theatre.

Display advertising is widely misunderstood. Critics point to low click-through rates and dismiss it. Sophisticated marketers understand display as a different beast: an awareness, audience-building and retargeting channel where view-through conversions, viewability and branded search lift tell the real story.

Measurement

Clicks are flattering. Incrementality is fundamental.

Six metrics we track every month so display always ladders back to business outcomes — not impression-counting theatre.

01

Viewability rate

MRC-compliant viewable impressions versus total served — the gate every other display metric depends on.

02

Click-through rate (CTR)

Tracked as a creative quality signal — never as the primary KPI. Display is not a click channel.

03

View-through conversions

Users who saw the ad and converted later without clicking — the metric that proves display is doing real work.

04

Frequency capping efficacy

Average impressions per user against the cap, and the diminishing-returns curve beyond optimal frequency.

05

Brand-lift survey deltas

Pre/post awareness, recall and consideration deltas measured via Google Brand Lift studies on qualifying budgets.

06

Incremental reach

Unique users reached by display that were not reachable through search or social — the real value of display in the media mix.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

What is the minimum budget for display advertising?
Display campaigns can be effective from ₹15,000 per month for retargeting. Brand awareness campaigns typically need higher budgets to build sufficient reach for meaningful frequency.
How do you prevent ads from appearing on low-quality sites?
We actively manage placement exclusions and use Google's brand safety controls. We also run regular placement reports to identify and exclude any placements that do not align with your brand.
Are display ads still worth it given low click-through rates?
Yes — when run correctly. Display is not a click-driving channel; it is an awareness, retargeting and view-through conversion channel. Measured against the right metrics (view-through conversions, brand lift, branded search lift), display delivers measurable returns. Measured against clicks alone, it always looks bad.
How do you ensure ads don't appear on harmful or low-quality sites?
Through multi-layer brand safety: Google's built-in content category exclusions, blacklist management of low-quality domains, third-party verification (DoubleVerify, IAS) for enterprise budgets, and regular placement reports to remove problematic sites discovered post-serve.
What's the minimum budget for a display campaign?
Meaningful display campaigns typically need ₹50,000+ per month to gather enough data for optimisation. Below that, statistical significance becomes elusive. Smaller budgets are better deployed in search PPC where intent makes every rupee work harder.
