DV Digital's real estate lead generation services are built around this extended journey: capturing high-intent search traffic, nurturing consideration-stage audiences on Meta, and converting enquiries through WhatsApp follow-up automation.

Real estate marketing in India is a unique discipline — long consideration cycles, high ticket sizes, multi-stakeholder decisions, and aggressive competition from listing platforms that intercept buyer intent. Generalist marketing agencies frequently fail in real estate because they apply consumer-funnel logic to a market where buyers behave more like B2B enterprise purchasers — researching for months, comparing dozens of options, and bringing partners and parents into final decisions.

As a real estate lead generation agency in India, DV Digital runs end-to-end marketing for residential, commercial, and luxury property developers. We have driven site visits, brochure downloads, and serious enquiries for projects across Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Delhi NCR — and we measure success in qualified site visits and bookings, not in lead-form submissions that look good in dashboards.

Lead Quality and Qualification for Real Estate

Real estate sales teams are notoriously sceptical about marketing-generated leads — and often with good reason. The volume-vs-quality tradeoff is steeper in real estate than in most categories because sales conversations consume so much time per lead. We invest heavily in qualification rigour:

Budget pre-qualification at lead capture

filtering tyre-kickers from serious buyers

Location and configuration matching

2BHK seekers don't get 4BHK leads, and vice versa

Buying timeline qualification

distinguishing now-buyers from 6-month researchers

Repeat enquiry deduplication

same buyer across channels counted once, not five times

Sales team feedback loops

every accepted/rejected lead refines campaign targeting

Cost per qualified site visit

the metric we report on, not raw cost per lead

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Which real estate segments do you work with?
Residential developers, commercial real estate, luxury properties, affordable housing, plotted developments, and real estate brokerages. Our targeting and messaging strategies are adapted to each segment's specific buyer profile.
Do you run campaigns on Housing.com and MagicBricks as well?
We specialise in Google Ads, Meta Ads, and SEO for direct lead generation. We can advise on property portal strategies but focus our management on owned channel campaigns.
How do you ensure leads are serious buyers and not just curious browsers?
We layer qualification at multiple stages: ad-level targeting (income-correlated audiences, location and behaviour filters), lead-form qualification (budget range, timeline, configuration preference), automated lead scoring before sales handoff, and continuous feedback loops with your sales team. Cost per qualified site visit, not per lead, is the metric that matters.
What's a typical cost per site visit in Indian real estate?
It varies by city, segment, and project pricing. Luxury projects often see ₹3,000–₹8,000 per qualified site visit. Mid-segment ₹1,200–₹3,500. Affordable housing ₹400–₹1,500. We benchmark realistic targets after auditing your project, location, and competitive landscape.
Can you market both pre-launch and ready-to-move-in inventory?
Yes — these require fundamentally different funnels. Pre-launch projects need awareness, education, and early-bird booking incentives over a 6–12 month build cycle. Ready-to-move inventory needs immediate booking conversion campaigns with site visit emphasis. We design appropriate strategies for each stage.
