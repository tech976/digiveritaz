FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you write SEO-optimised content?
Yes. All content is produced with keyword integration, semantic coverage, and proper heading structure to support organic search performance.
Can you write in a specific tone of voice?
Always. Every project begins with a tone of voice brief. We produce sample copy for approval before full content production begins.
Do you use AI tools to write content?
Our writers use AI as research and editing assistants — never as a writing replacement. Pure AI content struggles with original insight, expert voice, and Google's helpful content guidelines. Human writers handle structure, voice, expertise, and final craft. AI helps us deliver faster without compromising quality.
Can you write technical content for specialised industries?
Yes — our writer pods are organised by industry expertise. Finance content is written by writers with financial services background, healthcare by clinical writers, technical SaaS by writers with engineering exposure. We do not assign generalist writers to specialist topics.
What does content writing typically cost?
Pricing varies by format and complexity: blog posts ₹4,000–₹15,000 each; landing pages ₹15,000–₹40,000; case studies ₹20,000–₹50,000; pillar guides ₹25,000–₹75,000. Retainer engagements are typically more cost-effective than per-piece pricing.
