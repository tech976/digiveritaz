How we work

From voice intake to publish, measure, iterate — every month.

Five disciplines we run in lockstep so every piece earns rankings, reads like your brand and ladders back to a business outcome.

01

Audience & voice intake

We start with a discovery interview, an audience deep-dive and a documented brand voice — tone, vocabulary, sentence rhythm, do's and don'ts. Every writer briefed on this brand starts from the same single source of truth.

02

Topic strategy & briefs

Keyword and intent research, SERP analysis, pillar-cluster mapping and an editorial calendar your team can plan against. Every piece ships with a real strategic brief — not a Google Doc template with a few bullet points.

03

Draft, edit, fact-check

Industry-specialist writers draft from the brief; a senior editor reviews every piece against the brand voice document; a fact-check pass catches numbers, claims and citations before anything goes near publish.

04

SEO & conversion polish

Headings, meta, internal links, semantic coverage, schema and CTA placement tuned in a final polish pass — so the same piece serves both the search engine and the human ready to convert.

05

Publish, measure, iterate

Every month you receive a content performance review — what ranked, what converted, what underperformed and which existing pieces deserve a refresh next. Content compounds when it is governed, not just commissioned.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

Do you write SEO-optimised content?
Yes. All content is produced with keyword integration, semantic coverage, and proper heading structure to support organic search performance.
Can you write in a specific tone of voice?
Always. Every project begins with a tone of voice brief. We produce sample copy for approval before full content production begins.
Do you use AI tools to write content?
Our writers use AI as research and editing assistants — never as a writing replacement. Pure AI content struggles with original insight, expert voice, and Google's helpful content guidelines. Human writers handle structure, voice, expertise, and final craft. AI helps us deliver faster without compromising quality.
Can you write technical content for specialised industries?
Yes — our writer pods are organised by industry expertise. Finance content is written by writers with financial services background, healthcare by clinical writers, technical SaaS by writers with engineering exposure. We do not assign generalist writers to specialist topics.
What does content writing typically cost?
Pricing varies by format and complexity: blog posts ₹4,000–₹15,000 each; landing pages ₹15,000–₹40,000; case studies ₹20,000–₹50,000; pillar guides ₹25,000–₹75,000. Retainer engagements are typically more cost-effective than per-piece pricing.
