Most founders treat brand identity as something to fix later — a Canva logo, whatever colours look fine, and a promise to rebrand at scale. That decision compounds for years in every investor meeting and sales call.

We approach brand identity as a system, not a logo. The mark is the most visible piece, but the colour tokens, type pairings, photography direction, voice and application discipline are what actually make a brand memorable across every customer touchpoint.

For ambitious Indian brands, strategy-led identity work compounds in value — many of the identities we shipped in 2019 are still in active service today, because they were built from positioning down to pixel, not the other way around.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

What does a complete brand identity project cost?
Comprehensive brand engagements range from ₹3.5 lakhs for early-stage startups to ₹15+ lakhs for full-strategy rebrands of established companies. Pricing depends on scope — strategy depth, naming, application range, and guideline comprehensiveness.
How long does a brand identity project take?
Standard engagements run 8–14 weeks: 2–3 weeks for strategy and discovery, 3–4 weeks for design exploration and refinement, 2–3 weeks for application and guidelines, plus client review cycles. Aggressive timelines (5–6 weeks) are possible for focused engagements.
Do you also handle brand naming or only visual identity?
Both. Strategic brand naming — from category research through trademark and domain validation — is part of our service for new brand launches, sub-brands, and rebrands where the existing name is part of the problem.
Can you redesign just the logo without a full rebrand?
Yes — but we generally recommend against pure logo redesigns disconnected from broader brand strategy. A new logo without strategic clarity often creates brand inconsistency rather than fixing problems. We can scope focused logo refreshes when warranted.
What happens after the brand is delivered — do you stay involved?
Most clients engage us for ongoing brand stewardship — handling new applications, ensuring consistency, and evolving the brand over time. We also offer brand audit services 12 months post-launch to ensure adoption is on track and identify gaps.
