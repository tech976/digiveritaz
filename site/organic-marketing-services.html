FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How many pieces of content should I publish per month?
There is no single right answer — it depends on your goals, competitor cadence, and topic landscape. Typical engagements range from 4 long-form pieces per month for niche B2B to 12–20 pieces per month for competitive consumer categories. We recommend cadence based on what your category and search landscape actually rewards.
How long until content starts driving organic traffic?
Individual pieces typically begin ranking within 3–4 months for low-to-medium competition keywords. A complete content marketing programme — pillar guides, cluster posts, internal linking — usually shows compounding traffic growth from month 6 onward, with significant traffic from month 9–12.
Do you write content using AI tools or human writers?
Our content is written by human writers who use AI tools as research and editing assistants — never as replacement. Pure AI-generated content is increasingly recognisable, struggles to rank under Google's helpful content guidelines, and rarely converts. Our writers handle structure, voice, expert input, and final polish.
Will my industry experts need to be involved in content creation?
Some involvement is essential — typically 30–60 minutes per pillar piece for expert interviews where we extract unique insights, examples, and opinions. This is what makes your content rank-worthy and citation-worthy. We minimise your time investment while ensuring genuine expertise shines through.
What does content marketing typically cost?
Content marketing engagements range from ₹50,000 per month for focused blog programmes to ₹3,50,000+ per month for comprehensive engines including pillar guides, case studies, original research, and video scripts. We scope based on your goals and provide clear pricing upfront.
