Aesthetic & Weight Loss

1,556 Leads per Month

How Shape U Clinic generated 1,556 qualified treatment leads per month with a precision Meta funnel and instant WhatsApp automation.

Client
Shape U Clinic
Industry
Aesthetic & Weight Loss
Services
Lead Gen, SMM, CRO
Platforms
Meta, WhatsApp

The Challenge

Aesthetic and weight-loss marketing is crowded, skeptical and expensive. Shape U needed to cut through noise, pre-qualify aggressively, and protect CAC.

  • Generate high-quality treatment consultations in a competitive market
  • Reduce unqualified leads & improve clinic-visit ratio
  • Build trust in non-surgical fat reduction
  • Improve CAC

The Strategy

Meta precision targeting layered across cold, warm and hot audiences — with a 5–8 km clinic-radius overlay and income-based urban clusters.

  • Cold — weight loss, gym, slimming interests; age 25–45
  • Warm — website visitors, IG engagers, 50%+ video viewers
  • Hot — WhatsApp enquiries, existing leads, consultation no-shows
  • Lead qualification — 3-question qualifier + instant WhatsApp automation + Conversions API
  • Creative — problem-solution hooks (“Lost 7 cm in 1st session”), doctor-led videos, before/after funnels, scarcity offers
  • Trust engine — DM automation, 1-hour comment response SLA, patient testimonials

The Results

A high-velocity lead funnel paired with instant response — not a traffic dump into an inbox.
