9 Meraki Panvel Lead Generation

How tight geo-targeting and intent-led Google Search delivered qualified homebuyer leads for Tulsi Realty's 9 Meraki project.

Client
Tulsi Realty
Industry
Real Estate
Services
Pay Per Click
Platforms
Google Search

The Challenge

Panvel's residential market is crowded, CPCs are rising and broad campaigns tend to attract low-quality leads. Tulsi Realty needed location-specific, high-intent volume — without blowing CPL.

  • Generate qualified property leads for 9 Meraki Panvel
  • Target users actively searching locally for residential properties
  • Maintain a cost-effective CPL
  • Improve lead quality for the sales team
  • Expand project visibility among homebuyers

The Strategy

A segmented Google Search build with surgical geo-targeting and feedback-led optimisation.

  • Campaign architecture — separate brand, location and generic intent campaigns
  • Keyword focus — “flats in Panvel”, “1/2 BHK Panvel”, “property near Panvel station”
  • Ad optimisation — USP-led copy, “Book Site Visit” & “Enquire Now” CTAs
  • Landing pages — dedicated, trust-led, speed-optimised
  • Budget management — daily keyword-level monitoring and reallocation
  • Negatives & bid control to eliminate low-quality traffic

Tools

Google Ads (Search), GA4, Google Tag Manager, call tracking, lead form tracking.

The Results

A location-led PPC engine delivering site-visit-ready buyers for 9 Meraki Panvel.
