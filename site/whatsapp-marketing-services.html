FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is WhatsApp marketing legal in India?
Yes, when done correctly. WhatsApp Business API requires opt-in consent from recipients and approved message templates. We ensure full compliance with WhatsApp's policies and India's data protection guidelines.
What kind of businesses benefit most from WhatsApp marketing?
Real estate, financial services, healthcare, e-commerce, and education businesses see particularly strong results. Any business with significant lead follow-up or customer communication requirements will benefit.
What does WhatsApp Business API cost?
WhatsApp marketing has multiple cost components: BSP platform fees (typically ₹2,500–₹15,000 per month depending on volume tier), Meta conversation fees (₹0.50–₹1.00 per marketing conversation; transactional and authentication conversations have different rates), and agency management fees (₹25,000–₹1.5 lakh per month depending on scope). Setup costs typically range ₹25,000–₹75,000.
How quickly can we launch WhatsApp marketing?
Standard setup including BSP onboarding, Meta verification, phone number provisioning, template submission and approval, and integration with your existing systems takes 3–6 weeks. Template approvals from Meta typically take 24–72 hours per template. Once live, broadcast campaigns can launch within days; complex automation flows take 2–4 weeks to design, build, and test.
