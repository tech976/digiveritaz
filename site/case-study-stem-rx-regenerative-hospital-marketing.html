Trust-First Healthcare Marketing

How Stem RX built a credible, education-led digital engine for regenerative medicine — ₹142 CPL with 90% OTP-verified lead quality.

Client
Stem RX
Industry
Healthcare
Services
Performance, SMM, SEO, CRO
Platforms
Meta, Google, SEO

The Challenge

Regenerative medicine is a high-consideration segment. Stem RX didn't just need leads — they needed trust, authority and patient education to support a serious treatment decision.

  • Build trust in regenerative treatments
  • Increase medical camp registrations and hospital walk-ins
  • Position doctors as credible authorities
  • Educate patients on non-surgical regenerative alternatives

The Strategy

A four-pillar programme balancing performance, authority content, SEO and CRO.

  • Performance Marketing — education-led creatives for AVN, Osteoarthritis, Diabetes, Autism and 20+ conditions; doctor-led authority ads; geo-targeted camp registrations
  • Social Media Management — therapy explainers, surgery vs regenerative comparisons, doctor Q&As, myth vs fact, testimonials, quizzes
  • SEO — treatment-specific keywords with medical schema, condition-focused blog articles, local SEO & GBP optimisation
  • CRO landing page — strong ATF CTA, doctor credibility section, FAQ, WhatsApp click-to-chat, 3-field form, camp urgency countdown

The Results

A high-trust healthcare funnel that converts seriously considered treatments into booked appointments.
