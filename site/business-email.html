Google Workspace vs Microsoft 365 — How to Choose

Google Workspace — better for collaboration-heavy teams, simpler administration, native cloud workflow

Microsoft 365 — better for businesses with Microsoft Office workflows, Excel-heavy teams, or enterprise compliance requirements

Both deliver core functionality (email, calendar, storage, video) at competitive prices

Migration between them is possible but disruptive — make the right choice from the start

Hybrid setups exist but multiply administrative complexity unnecessarily for most businesses

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better — Google Workspace or Microsoft 365?
Both are excellent. Google Workspace is generally preferred by marketing and creative teams who value Gmail's interface and Google Docs collaboration. Microsoft 365 is often preferred by businesses relying heavily on Excel, Word, and Teams. We advise based on your existing workflows.
Can you migrate our existing emails to a new provider?
Yes. We manage full email migrations including contacts and calendar data where applicable — ensuring no historical correspondence is lost in the transition.
Should we choose Google Workspace or Microsoft 365?
Google Workspace tends to win for cloud-native, collaboration-heavy teams that share documents constantly and value simpler administration. Microsoft 365 wins when your team uses heavy Excel workflows, has historical Office investment, or operates in industries with Microsoft-centric compliance requirements. Both are excellent — the answer depends on your actual workflow, not generic agency preference.
How long does email migration take?
Standard migrations to Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 take 2–6 weeks depending on team size and email volume. Phase 1 is account setup and domain configuration (3–5 days). Phase 2 is parallel mail flow setup and testing (1 week). Phase 3 is staged user migrations with email history transfer (1–4 weeks depending on team size). We minimise disruption by overlapping the phases.
What does business email cost?
Google Workspace plans range from ₹125/user/month (Business Starter) to ₹1,500/user/month (Business Plus and Enterprise). Microsoft 365 plans range from ₹136/user/month (Basic) to ₹1,650/user/month (Apps for Business and above). Setup and migration services typically range from ₹15,000 for small teams to ₹2 lakhs for complex migrations of 50+ users.
