We don't sell impressions. We build paid-media programmes that work as accountable growth engines — every rupee of spend tied to a click, a lead or a sale.

Performance marketing is digital marketing held to the standard most marketing should be held to: every rupee of spend tied to a measurable outcome. CPC, CPA, ROAS — every metric is calculable, every channel is comparable, every campaign decision can be defended with data rather than opinion. Done properly, it is the most accountable form of marketing ever invented.

As a performance marketing agency in India, DV Digital runs full-funnel programmes across Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, Amazon and emerging platforms — managing multi-crore monthly spend across consumer, B2B, e-commerce and lead-gen verticals. Our discipline is focused on what actually moves business outcomes, not the metrics that look impressive in slide decks.

How we work

From audit to scale — every rupee accountable.

Five disciplines we run in lockstep so paid spend compounds into revenue instead of disappearing into platform UI.

01

Audit & baseline

We start by auditing your existing accounts — tracking accuracy, account structure, conversion setup, attribution model, server-side tagging and historical performance benchmarks. You leave the first 14 days with a documented baseline ROAS, CAC and CVR per channel so every future change is measured against truth, not vibes.

02

Creative & offer strategy

Performance is 60% creative now — the bidding algorithms have closed the gap on everything else. We build a monthly creative slate of UGC reels, founder content, static iterations and offer-led hooks tested for thumbstop, hook-rate and conversion velocity, not aesthetics.

03

Channel mix + budget allocation

We size Google, Meta, LinkedIn, Amazon and programmatic against your funnel stage, AOV and margin profile — then map weekly budgets to where conversion intent is strongest. The mix is rebalanced weekly based on incremental performance, not last-click attribution alone.

04

Launch, test, scale

Campaigns launch with a structured test matrix — creative × audience × bid strategy × landing page. Winners are scaled aggressively, losers killed fast, learnings fed back into the creative slate. We don't run the same ad for six months hoping it works — we treat the account like a portfolio of bets.

05

Reporting & incrementality

Every month you receive a report covering blended ROAS, CAC, MER, contribution margin and incrementality test results — alongside a war-room call to review what worked, what didn't, and exactly how the next 30 days of spend will be sharpened. No black-box dashboards.

Measurement

Clicks flatter you. Revenue funds you.

Six metrics we report on every month so paid spend always ladders back to business outcomes — not platform UI screenshots.

01

Blended ROAS

Total attributed revenue divided by total ad spend across every channel — the only ROAS number a CFO actually cares about.

02

Customer Acquisition Cost

Fully-loaded CAC including platform fees and creative cost — tracked by cohort, channel and campaign.

03

Average Order Value

Tracked alongside basket size and SKU mix — the lever most agencies ignore but the one that quietly fixes ROAS.

04

Contribution margin

Revenue minus COGS, shipping and ad spend — the only number that tells you if scaling actually makes you money.

05

Marketing Efficiency Ratio (MER)

Total revenue ÷ total marketing spend — the blended-attribution number that survives iOS, cookies and AI agents.

06

Incrementality test results

Geo-holdouts and conversion-lift studies that surface the true marginal contribution of each channel — not just last-click ego.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they hand over media.

What metrics do you track for performance marketing?
Core metrics: cost per lead, cost per acquisition, return on ad spend, conversion rate and click-through rate. We also track pipeline contribution, contribution margin and revenue attribution where CRM integration is in place — so the report ties spend to revenue, not just platform UI numbers.
How is performance marketing different from traditional advertising?
Traditional advertising charges for placement or reach regardless of outcome. Performance marketing charges based on measurable actions — clicks, leads or sales — making ROI directly calculable and campaigns directly accountable. You can defend every decision with data instead of opinion.
What's the difference between performance marketing and digital marketing?
Performance marketing is a subset of digital marketing focused exclusively on measurable, ROI-accountable activity — paid acquisition, direct-response campaigns and channels where every rupee is tied to an outcome. Digital marketing is broader, including brand-building, content marketing, SEO and PR where direct measurement is harder. Most healthy strategies combine performance with brand investment, not choose one over the other.
What's the minimum budget for meaningful performance marketing?
Across all channels, ₹2 lakhs/month is roughly the floor where performance marketing economics start working — below that, agency fees consume too much of spend and machine-learning algorithms have too little data. Single-channel programmes can work at ₹1 lakh/month for tightly-targeted campaigns. Larger budgets unlock multi-channel optimisation and faster learning cycles.
How is performance marketing priced?
Either as percentage of spend (typically 10–18% for accounts above ₹3 lakhs/month) or flat retainers (₹50,000–₹3 lakhs/month) for smaller or strategy-heavy engagements. Some agencies offer hybrid models with performance bonuses tied to ROAS or CPA targets. We're transparent about which model applies and why for each engagement — so you always know what you're paying for.
