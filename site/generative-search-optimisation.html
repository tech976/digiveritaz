AI-powered search tools like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, and Perplexity are changing how people find information. Instead of clicking ten blue links, users increasingly receive synthesised answers generated directly by AI. Brands not optimised for generative search environments risk losing visibility as AI answers replace traditional search clicks.

Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) is the emerging discipline of making your brand, content, and expertise visible and credible within AI-generated responses — and DV Digital is building this capability alongside our established SEO practice.

Search behaviour is undergoing the largest structural shift since the rise of mobile. Increasingly, users get answers from AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude rather than clicking through to websites. The blue-link era is ending, and brands that depend exclusively on traditional search rankings are about to discover their visibility was always conditional on user behaviour that's now changing. Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) is the discipline of making your brand visible, cited, and recommended within AI-generated responses — and it requires both extending traditional SEO and adopting new practices specific to how AI systems retrieve and synthesise information.

As a Generative Engine Optimisation services provider in India, DV Digital helps Indian brands establish presence in AI search responses across Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Claude, and emerging platforms. We integrate GEO with traditional SEO, content marketing, and brand authority programmes — because GEO is not a replacement for those disciplines but an evolution of them.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEO and how is it different from SEO?
SEO optimises content to rank in traditional search engine results pages. GEO optimises content and brand signals to appear in AI-generated search responses from tools like Google AI Overviews and ChatGPT Search. Both are increasingly important and overlapping disciplines.
Is it possible to track performance in AI search results?
Tracking AI search visibility is still evolving. We use brand mention monitoring, systematic prompt testing across AI search tools, and Google Search Console data to assess and continuously improve your visibility in generative search environments.
Should we invest in GEO now or wait until it matures?
Now — early movers are establishing positioning that will be hard to dislodge. AI search is already material in some categories (B2B SaaS, professional services, healthcare research) and growing rapidly across all categories. The cost of starting now is low and the compounding benefit is high; the cost of waiting is permanently visible competitors who got there first while you weren't looking.
