FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

Do you build WordPress sites from scratch or use themes?
We build custom WordPress themes from scratch for clients who need a truly unique design. We also customise premium themes for projects where a faster turnaround is needed.
Do you provide WordPress maintenance?
Yes. Our WordPress maintenance plans cover monthly core and plugin updates, security scanning, daily backups, uptime monitoring, and minor content updates.
Should we use a WordPress theme or a custom build?
It depends on budget, requirements, and how unique the design needs to be. Themes work for early-stage brands with standard requirements and tight budgets. Custom builds make sense once your brand is established, design distinctiveness matters, or theme limitations are forcing compromises. We recommend honestly based on actual situation, not from agency preference for higher-fee custom work.
What does ongoing WordPress maintenance involve?
Core, theme, and plugin updates with testing before deployment. Daily backups with off-site storage. Security monitoring and threat response. Performance monitoring and optimisation. Uptime monitoring with alert response. Content updates and minor design changes. Most maintenance retainers run ₹15,000–₹75,000 per month depending on site complexity and update frequency.
What does a WordPress site cost to build?
Theme-based marketing sites: ₹1.5–6 lakhs depending on customisation. Custom WordPress builds with bespoke themes: ₹5–15 lakhs. WooCommerce stores: ₹4–18 lakhs depending on catalogue and integrations. Headless WordPress with React/Next.js frontend: ₹12–35 lakhs. Custom proposals after a discovery call.
