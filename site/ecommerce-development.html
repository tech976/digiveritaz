Measurement

Six KPIs that actually decide if a store is working.

We report against the same six numbers every month. Vanity dashboards skipped — these are the inputs and outputs that move GMV and contribution margin.

01

Page speed (LCP)

Mobile LCP under 2.5s as a non-negotiable. Every additional second drops conversion 7–10% on Indian mobile networks.

02

Checkout conversion

Add-to-cart to order-placed rate per device, per traffic source — the single highest-leverage number in any e-commerce P&L.

03

Average order value

AOV per traffic source and per collection — the bundling, recommendation and threshold-shipping levers that lift ROAS without raising bids.

04

Repeat purchase rate

30/60/90-day repeat rate by acquisition cohort — the only honest signal of whether your store has product-market fit, not just paid traffic.

05

Cart abandonment recovery

Revenue recovered via email, WhatsApp and browser push flows — typically 8–15% of otherwise-lost GMV with a clean retention stack.

06

GMV growth

Month-on-month GMV with channel attribution — what the store is actually contributing to the business, not just sessions and bounce rate.

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they sign.

Which e-commerce platforms do you build on?
Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and custom-built e-commerce platforms. Platform recommendations are based on your catalogue size, team capabilities, and growth plans.
Do you migrate existing e-commerce stores to new platforms?
Yes. We manage full e-commerce migrations including product data, customer data, order history, and URL structure — minimising SEO impact during the transition.
Should we build on Shopify or WooCommerce?
Shopify usually wins for most D2C brands — faster setup, better app ecosystem, less operational overhead, predictable platform costs. WooCommerce wins when you have existing WordPress investments, content-heavy commerce, or specific customisations Shopify doesn't support. We recommend platform after understanding your actual situation, not from agency familiarity.
Can you migrate our existing e-commerce store to a new platform?
Yes — migrations across platforms (Magento to Shopify, WooCommerce to Shopify Plus, custom to headless) are a regular service. We handle catalogue migration, customer data transfer, order history preservation, URL redirects for SEO continuity, and integration reconnection. Migrations typically take 8-16 weeks depending on catalogue size and customisation.
What does an e-commerce store cost to build?
Standard Shopify stores with 1-3 product collections: ₹3-8 lakhs. Mid-complexity stores with custom themes and integrations: ₹6-18 lakhs. Shopify Plus or Magento builds with extensive customisation: ₹15-50+ lakhs. Headless commerce builds with custom frontends: ₹25 lakhs to over a crore. Ongoing maintenance retainers from ₹25,000-2 lakhs per month.
