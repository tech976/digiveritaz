Building an e-commerce store today is technically easy — Shopify, WooCommerce, and dozens of platforms let almost anyone launch a store in days. Building an e-commerce store that converts at competitive rates, scales past initial traction, and operates without constant operational firefighting is much harder. Most stores we audit have technical, conversion, and operational issues that quietly cap revenue at multiples below what the brand could be doing.

As an e-commerce development company in India, DV Digital builds and rebuilds e-commerce stores engineered for revenue rather than just functionality. We work across Shopify, Shopify Plus, WooCommerce, Magento, and headless commerce architectures — and we choose the right platform for the actual brand requirements rather than defaulting to whatever's familiar.

E-Commerce Platforms We Build On

03

Shopify & Shopify Plus

Best-in-class for most D2C brands and growing e-commerce businesses. Shopify Plus for enterprise scale with high SKU counts, multi-region operations, or B2B+D2C hybrid models.

04

WooCommerce

Strong choice for brands with existing WordPress investments, content-heavy commerce, or specific customisation needs. Lower platform costs but higher operational responsibility.

05

Magento (Adobe Commerce)

Enterprise-grade platform for complex catalogues, B2B commerce, and brands with sophisticated personalisation or workflow requirements.

06

Headless Commerce Architectures

Decoupled commerce backends (Shopify, BigCommerce, commercetools) with custom React or Next.js frontends for brands prioritising performance and design flexibility.

07

Custom E-Commerce Builds

For unique business models — subscription commerce, marketplace platforms, or specialised vertical commerce — custom builds when off-the-shelf platforms don't fit.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Which e-commerce platforms do you build on?
Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and custom-built e-commerce platforms. Platform recommendations are based on your catalogue size, team capabilities, and growth plans.
Do you migrate existing e-commerce stores to new platforms?
Yes. We manage full e-commerce migrations including product data, customer data, order history, and URL structure — minimising SEO impact during the transition.
Should we build on Shopify or WooCommerce?
Shopify usually wins for most D2C brands — faster setup, better app ecosystem, less operational overhead, predictable platform costs. WooCommerce wins when you have existing WordPress investments, content-heavy commerce, or specific customisations Shopify doesn't support. We recommend platform after understanding your actual situation, not from agency familiarity.
Can you migrate our existing e-commerce store to a new platform?
Yes — migrations across platforms (Magento to Shopify, WooCommerce to Shopify Plus, custom to headless) are a regular service. We handle catalogue migration, customer data transfer, order history preservation, URL redirects for SEO continuity, and integration reconnection. Migrations typically take 8-16 weeks depending on catalogue size and customisation.
What does an e-commerce store cost to build?
Standard Shopify stores with 1-3 product collections: ₹3-8 lakhs. Mid-complexity stores with custom themes and integrations: ₹6-18 lakhs. Shopify Plus or Magento builds with extensive customisation: ₹15-50+ lakhs. Headless commerce builds with custom frontends: ₹25 lakhs to over a crore. Ongoing maintenance retainers from ₹25,000-2 lakhs per month.
