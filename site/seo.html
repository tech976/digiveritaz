Our 4-Phase SEO Process: Audit, Strategy, Execution, Authority

Every SEO engagement at DV Digital follows a structured four-phase framework. This is the same process we have used to take 120+ keywords to Page 1 of Google for our clients.

01
Phase 01

Technical & Content Audit

We surface crawlability issues, Core Web Vitals failures, thin content pages, broken internal links, duplicate content, indexation problems, and any algorithmic penalties suppressing your rankings. Every audit produces a prioritised fix list ranked by impact and effort, so you know exactly what to fix first and why.

02
Phase 02

Keyword & Search Intent Strategy

We build keyword strategies rooted in search intent — targeting visitors ready to buy, enquire, or convert. Our research goes beyond search volume to map informational, navigational, commercial, and transactional intent across the buyer journey, ensuring content earns rankings for queries that drive revenue.

03
Phase 03

On-Page & Content Execution

Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal linking architecture, structured data, image optimisation, and content depth are aligned with how Google evaluates topical authority. We rewrite, expand, or restructure existing content and create new pillar content to fill semantic gaps in your topic coverage.

04
Phase 04

Authority & Off-Page Building

High-quality editorial backlinks from credible Indian and international publications, digital PR placements, brand citation building, and strategic guest contributions lift domain authority sustainably. We never engage in PBN links, comment spam, or any tactic that risks a manual action.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to show results in 2026?
Most clients see meaningful traction within 3–4 months — improved rankings on long-tail queries, growing organic traffic, and the first qualified leads from search. Significant ranking gains for competitive primary keywords typically materialise between months 6 and 9. SEO is a compounding investment — by month 12, results usually exceed paid acquisition on cost-per-lead basis.
What does a typical SEO engagement with DV Digital cost?
Engagements start from approximately ₹35,000 per month for small businesses and scale based on keyword competitiveness, site complexity, and the scope of content and link building. We provide a custom proposal after a free initial audit so you see exactly what is included and what results are realistic for your situation.
Do you offer guaranteed Page 1 rankings?
No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithms are too dynamic, and any agency promising guarantees is using tactics that will eventually trigger penalties. What we do guarantee is a transparent process, ethical white-hat methodology, measurable progress reporting, and a documented track record of taking 120+ keywords to Page 1.
Will my SEO work survive Google algorithm updates?
Yes — because our methodology is built around what Google's algorithm updates are designed to reward: genuine expertise, authoritative content, strong technical foundations, and earned authority. Clients on our retainer have consistently held or improved rankings through every major core update over the past three years.
Can you handle SEO for an e-commerce site with 10,000+ products?
Yes. Large-catalogue e-commerce SEO is one of our specialisations. We have managed sites with 50,000+ SKUs and understand the technical considerations — crawl budget, faceted navigation, programmatic content, internal linking at scale, and category page authority distribution.
