Four phases. One compounding asset.

Keyword & search-intent strategy

FAQs

Questions teams ask before they start SEO.

How long does SEO take to show results in 2026?
Most clients see meaningful traction within 3–4 months — improved rankings on long-tail queries, growing organic traffic, and the first qualified leads from search. Significant ranking gains for competitive primary keywords typically materialise between months 6 and 9. SEO is a compounding investment — by month 12, results usually exceed paid acquisition on cost-per-lead basis.
What does a typical SEO engagement with DV Digital cost?
Engagements start from approximately ₹35,000 per month for small businesses and scale based on keyword competitiveness, site complexity, and the scope of content and link building. We provide a custom proposal after a free initial audit so you see exactly what's included and what results are realistic for your situation.
Do you offer guaranteed Page 1 rankings?
No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithms are too dynamic, and any agency promising guarantees is using tactics that will eventually trigger penalties. What we do guarantee is a transparent process, ethical white-hat methodology, measurable progress reporting, and a documented track record of taking 120+ keywords to Page 1.
Will my SEO work survive Google algorithm updates?
Yes — because our methodology is built around what Google's algorithm updates are designed to reward: genuine expertise, authoritative content, strong technical foundations and earned authority. Clients on our retainer have consistently held or improved rankings through every major core update over the past three years.
Can you handle SEO for an e-commerce site with 10,000+ products?
Yes. Large-catalogue e-commerce SEO is one of our specialisations. We've managed sites with 50,000+ SKUs and understand the technical considerations — crawl budget, faceted navigation, programmatic content, internal linking at scale and category page authority distribution.
